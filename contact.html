Frequently Asked Questions

1. How far in advance should we book your services?

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance, especially for weddings during peak season, to ensure our availability. However, don't hesitate to contact us for last-minute inquiries.

2. What happens if it rains on our outdoor shoot day?

We always monitor the weather closely. If rain is forecasted, we can either reschedule to a mutually convenient date or switch to a beautiful indoor location. Your booking advance is always secure.

3. When will we receive our photos and videos?

You will receive a sneak peek of a few edited images within a week. The full gallery of photos is typically delivered within 4-6 weeks, and cinematic films may take up to 8-10 weeks, depending on the complexity of the project.

4. Do you travel for destination weddings?

Absolutely! We love capturing stories in new and beautiful locations. We have experience covering events across Goa and are available for destination weddings throughout India and beyond. Travel and accommodation charges apply.

5. Can we customize a photography package?

Yes, of course. While we offer standard packages, we understand every event is unique. We are happy to create a custom package tailored specifically to your needs, budget, and vision.
